Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about our Minecraft hosting services? Check out our FAQ for answers.

When making a purchase, our system will automatically register you, create your account, and provide you with your credentials. Subsequently, it will allocate the services you have acquired. Not a customer but want to access a friend's server? Your friend should add you in the 'Users' section on their control panel.

At the moment, our only payment method is PayPal. We will be adding more payment methods in the near future. Currently, this is the safest payment method for both you as the buyer and us as a company. Before making a purchase, we strongly recommend that you read our Terms of Service.

To reset your password, you'll need to go to our control panel, in the login section. There, you'll click on the 'Forgot my password' link, fill out the form, and wait for an email with further instructions to be sent to you.

You can contact our customer support in various ways, either via email or through Discord. For email support, please send us an email at 'support@netbliss.net.' If you prefer Discord, you can join our Discord server, navigate to the 'tickets' section, send a ticket in the channel, and fill out your request there.

The delivery time of our services depends on the load of our systems and is usually immediate but may vary. Upon completing your purchase, you will receive an email with your panel username and password. Once inside the panel, you will find the services you have acquired.
